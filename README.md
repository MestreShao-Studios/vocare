# Vocare

**Turn any page or screenshot into spaced-repetition flashcards — AI runs on your phone.**

Snap a photo of what you are reading (or share a screenshot straight into Vocare), tap the words you want, and Vocare builds a flashcard for each — meaning, a natural example sentence in the language you are studying, an explanation, and multiple-choice review. FSRS-6 spaced repetition then schedules them so they actually stick.

For language learners reading anywhere — a book, a website, a message, a sign.

## How it works

1. **Capture** — photograph a page, or Share a screenshot from Photos, Safari, Messages, anywhere.
2. **Select** — tap the words you want with Apple Live Text.
3. **Cards** — meaning + a new example sentence in your study language + explanation + multiple-choice, generated on-device.
4. **Review** — FSRS-6 spaced repetition surfaces each word right before you would forget it.

## On-device AI — private by design

Vocare Basic cards are generated by **Google Gemma**, running entirely on your phone (iOS 26+). Nothing leaves the device — no account, no cloud, works offline. On iOS 18 and earlier, Basic falls back to Apple on-device Translation.
